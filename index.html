Welcome to the Lost and Found System

Welcome to our Lost and Found system! This platform is designed to help users easily report lost or found items in a designated area, such as a campus or building. The system integrates interactive maps and forms to efficiently manage and track lost items, helping both those who have lost something and those who have found an item to reunite them with their belongings.

The page includes:

  • A form to report lost items, including details about the item, location, and time.
  • A form for reporting found items, where you can select from a list of previously reported lost items.
  • Interactive campus map to visualize item locations and easily find lost and found items in specific buildings or areas.
  • A real-time list of lost and found items, displayed for easy reference.

To begin using the system, you can navigate to the respective forms for reporting items or use the map to explore the locations where items may have been lost or found.
